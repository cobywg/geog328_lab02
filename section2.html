Biography


Tommy G once worked in the corporate world but always asked for more. After college, he decided to enter the corporate world because he was unsure of what else to do. Eventually, Tommy started making prank videos on YouTube. After posting for awhile no videos or content seemed to be sticking for Tommy. After a few years at his corporate job, he decided to create a documentary. He made a video with the Kia Boys and it went completely viral. This skyrocketed Tommy's career. Now Tommy has a big following allowing him to quit his corporate job to be able to make documentaries around the world.
