Intro


Tommy G is a popular YouTuber who travels around the United States and reports on real stories affecting different cities. He has 1 million subscribers and posts weekly documentaries. Tommy G is an independent journalist and has no media affiliations. Furthermore, Tommy has received over 100 million views and only became popular within the last year.



Tommy G's big break


Tommy G got his break when posting the Kia Boys videos documentary about a year ago. Tommy was interviewed by the media and his channel got a boost in viewership. Tommy was able to keep his audience and has shown he is also able to grow his audience since he went viral. Tommy G has continued to post weekly and he keeps receiving more and more views.
